# **App Name**: TruckLog

## Core Features:

- Secure Login: User authentication with email and password.
- Daily Log: Weekly log for entering hours and kilometers driven per day. User must choose a status: worked, sick, free (vrij), or parental leave (ouderschapsverlof). If other than worked, fields are disabled. Entry cannot be zero.
- Weekly Overview: Displays hours / kilometer entry for the current week, with editing allowed until Monday 12:00 AM. 
- Monthly Report: Monthly overview of total hours worked. Display hours of all of the previous weeks of the month, combined.
- Lock Past Weeks: Prevents editing of past weeks after Monday at 12:00 AM.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Strong blue (#3498db), symbolizing reliability and professionalism suitable for the transport sector.
- Background color: Light grey (#ecf0f1) to ensure a clean and accessible interface.
- Accent color: Orange (#e67e22), provides a contrast for key interactive elements such as 'save' buttons.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a sans-serif font that's both modern and easily readable.
- Use icons representing time (clock), distance (road), and status (calendar) to aid quick visual recognition.
- Clean, structured layout to minimize errors when inputting weekly information.
